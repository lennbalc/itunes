FLOWER BOY

Tyler The Creator

LEARN MORE

Tyler Gregory Okonma, better known as Tyler, The Creator, Wolf Haley, or on certain days, DJ Stank Daddy, is a rapper and record producer from California. He first received major recognition in the hip-hop community in the early 2010’s as the co-founder of rap group Odd Future.

Known for his explosive personality and off-the wall lyrical content, Tyler released his first mixtape, Bastard in 2009. Subsequent releases were his first studio album Goblin, in 2011 under XL Recordings, Wolf in 2013 under Odd Future Records, Cherry Bomb in 2015, and his latest release Flower Boy in 2017 under Columbia Records.

SATURATION II

Brockhampton

LEARN MORE

BROCKHAMPTON is an LA-based fast emerging musical and artistic collective (or “boy band”, as they’ve called themselves) formed in San Marcos, Texas in 2015.

Featuring a roster of creatives, BROCKHAMPTON boasts musicians, graphic designers, producers, and videographers who are combining their talents to grace us with a mix of content.

In 2016, they released their debut mixtape ALL-AMERICAN TRASH. It was in 2017 that the group started to gain popularity, when they started releasing singles to promote their debut album SATURATION (released in June, 2017), followed by SATURATION II (released in August, 2017) and SATURATION III (set to release in October, 2017).

MALIBU

Anderson .Paak

LEARN MORE

Brandon Paak Anderson, better known by his stage name Anderson .Paak, is a singer, songwriter, producer and drummer from Oxnard, California.

Originally known as Breezy Lovejoy, .Paak released his debut EP, Violets Are Blue. 2015 was a standout year for .Paak thanks to his guest features. Appearing on 6 songs on Dr. Dre’s Compton, as well as two songs on The Game’s The Documentary 2.5, .Paak’s popularity skyrocketed.

In January of 2016, .Paak released his second album, Malibu. The album, sixteen tracks in length, is .Paak’s strongest work to date, showing the growth he’s made since his Breezy Lovejoy days.
